Welcome !

One of the principal values of travelling is that, it breaks the monotony of life and work. Life, for most people, is a mad rush from one place to another, from one activity to another, trying to gather as much as possible.


In this pro­cess, people tend to forget, who they are and what they are. There is no time to ponder and wonder. They tend to forget the values of life. Travelling is a time when people relax, reflect and ponder. Most people, after a pleasant travel, re­turn home with a fresh outlook, new zeal and a better deter­mination.

Places

Sirsi

Sirsi is a city in the Uttara Kannada district in the Indian state of Karnataka. It is the largest city in North Canara district.Sirsi is a popular destination for trekkers and nature lovers alike, owing to its idyllic location in the Western Ghats. The city is surrounded by lush green forest and several waterfalls.

Dandeli

Dandeli is a town in Uttara Kannada district of Karnataka, India, in the Western Ghats region in North Karnataka. Dandeli is a natural habitat for wildlife including tigers, leopards, black panthers, elephants, deer and bears. It is the second largest wildlife sanctuary in Karnataka and birds.

Murudeshwara

Murudeshwar is a town in Bhatkal Taluk of Uttara Kannada district in the state of Karnataka, India. Murudeshwar is another name of the Hindu god Shiva. Famous for the world's second-tallest Shiva statue, the town lies on the coast of the Arabian Sea and is also famous for the Murudeshwar Temple.
